What is Disaster Recovery?

Disaster recovery within any organisation or business is its ability to recover and respond to events that affect the operations of the organisation, within a Datacenter the disaster recovery plan and systems are even more important as datacentres are designed to provide constant service. If this service, the Datacenter encounters interruptions, or outages it will lead to significant losses not only for the operators of the datacentres but also the potentially thousands of businesses which rely on the servers within the Datacenter. Disaster recovery is achieved in datacentres through systems that prevent downtime, these systems can involve having a disaster recovery plan which details what procedures must be taken in a disaster event such as what data is replicated to an off-site facility.
